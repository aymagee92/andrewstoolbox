Federal Excel Templates

Income

Annuity/Pension Exclusion Calculator ? This webpage has:
(1) Enter taxpayer information to determine how much of their annuity/pension is taxable.
(2) Ability to print off a schedule for reference in future years.
Last Updated: updated during tax season by cotaxaide.org/tools

Business Activity Codes ? This is a webpage that allows you to quickly click through the various business activity codes to get the relevant number for the Schedule C/E
Last Updated: updated during tax season by cotaxaide.org/tools

Does a Dependent Need to File Federal? ? This excel has:
(1) Income test to determine whether a dependent is required to file their own Federal return or not.
(2) List of non-income related situations where a dependent would be required to file a Federal return.
Last Updated: 05/30/2022

Dependent Qualification Calculator ? This web-based tool is a quick way to find out:
(1) Is a person eligible to be claimed as a dependent
(2) What Deductions/Credits the potential dependent qualifies for.
Note: If trying to explain to taxpayer, I recommend using the excel-based checklists I create. However, this is a great tool for self-use.
Last Updated: updated during tax season by cotaxaide.org/tools

IRA Form 8606 Exclusion Calcuator ? Form 8606 is needed in 3 situations listed below. This tool is designed to calculate the taxable vs. non-taxable portion for Part 1 situations.
........(Part 1) Non-deductible contributions/distributions to/from a Traditional/SIMPLE/SEP that contains a non-deductible contribution.
........(Part 2) Rollover of Traditional/SIMPLE/SEP IRA to Roth IRA, and
........(Part 3) Distribution from Roth IRA
Last Updated: updated during tax season by cotaxaide.org/tools

Sales Tax Estimator on IRS.gov ? This is the official IRS.gov tool for esimating your sales tax paid for the year. This is only needed for Schedule A Line 5 if client is itemizing deductions.
Last Updated: updated by the IRS

Schedule A Line 5 Calculation ? Calculates Schedule A Line 5
Last Updated: 5/29/2022

Social Security Taxation Calculator ? This excel workbook has:
(1) Walkthrough for calculating taxability of taxpayer's social security. Helps when explaining to taxpayer.
(2) Figures for the Social Security Benefits Worksheet. Helps to audit the software.
(3) Ability to see other scenerios. Useful for planning purposes.
Last Updated: 05/29/2022

State Refund Taxability Calculator ? This is a webpage that calculates the taxability of a state refund if you itemized in the prior year.
Last Updated: updated during tax season by cotaxaide.org/tools

Statutory Employee, Non-Statutory Employee, and Independent Contractor Info ? This excel defines and shows the forms to be filed for the 4 different types of worker classifications:
(1) Common Law Employee
(2) Independent Contractor
(3) Statutory Employee
(4) Non-Statutory Employee
Last Updated: 05/30/2022

Deductions

Standard Deduction & Personal Exemption Calculator ? This excel workbook has:
(1) Standard deduction & personal exemption data back to 2017
(2) Enter taxpayer data and compare standard deduction with different filing statuses and prior tax years.
Last Updated: 05/28/2022

Credits

Earned Income Tax Credit Excel ? This excel workbook has:
(1) Checklist to determine eligibility.
(2) Graph to visualize the EIC and see where the taxpayer falls.
Last Updated: 05/28/2022

Education Credit Optimizer ? This is a web-based tool that allows you to enter your tax information of the taxpayer and all potential dependents to determine which education credits would give you the greatest tax benefit.
Last Updated: updated during tax season by cotaxaide.org/tools

Determining Tax

Federal Tax Calculator - Basic ? This excel workbook is used to visualize and explain how the Federal 1040 tax (called "tax" on 1040) is calculated from Taxable Income.
This is not used to calculate the final tax amount (called "total tax" on 1040).
It does not consider higher capital gains rates for 1250 or collectibles.
Last Updated: 05/30/2022

Withholding Visual ? This excel workbook is used to:
(1) Visualize withholding percentages on each income document for both federal and one state.
(2) See overall withholding percent and how it compares to effective tax rate.
Last Updated: 06/03/2022

Expired Programs

COVID Programs ? This excel workbook contains:
(1) COVID Stimulus Calculator (a.k.a. Recovery Rebate Credit)
(2) Employee Retention Credit Calculator
(3) Sick and Family Leave Calculator
(4) Trump's Payroll Tax Deferral Calculator
Note that stimulus was live tested and confirmed to be correct. The others were never live tested and could contain calculation errors.
Last Updated: 03/08/2022
